ID (Pokédex Number): This is the unique number assigned to each Pokémon in the official Pokédex.
Name: The name of the Pokémon.
HP: How much health a Pokémon has before it gets knocked out.
Attack: This stat shows how strong a Pokémon’s physical moves are.
Defense: This tells you how much Pokémon can take physical hits without getting hurt too much.
Sp. Attack: This number indicates how powerful a Pokémon’s special moves are.
Sp. Defense: This stat measures how well a Pokémon can resist damage from special moves.
Speed: A higher speed means the Pokémon is more likely to attack first during battles.
Base_Stats: This is the total of all the key stats (HP, Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed) combined, giving a quick overall idea of the Pokémon’s power.
Weaknesses: Indicates how much damage certain type moves can do.
Includes: Normal, Fire, Water, Electric, Grass, Ice, Fighting, Poison, Ground, Flying, Psychic, Bug, Rock, Ghost, Dragon, Dark, Steel, and Fairy type.
height_inches: The Pokémon’s height measured in inches.
height_meters: The Pokémon’s height measured in meters.
weight_pounds: The weight of the Pokémon in pounds.
weight_kilograms: The weight of the Pokémon in kilograms.
capturing_rate: This number gives an idea of how easy or hard it is to catch the Pokémon.
gender_male_ratio: This shows the percentage chance that the Pokémon is male. (Can be null)
egg_steps: The number of steps needed for an egg to hatch into a Pokémon.
egg_cycles: The number of egg cycles required for the Pokémon to hatch.
abilities: A list of special skills or powers that the Pokémon has passively.
Type 1: The primary elemental type of the Pokémon, i.e. Water, Fire, or Grass.
Type 2: If the Pokémon has a second type, it’s listed here.
Classification_info: This gives a description for the Pokémon (like “Seed Pokémon” or “Flame Pokémon”).
Forms: This tells you if the Pokémon's different forms or appearances.
gen: Which generation the Pokémon first appeared in.
Is_Legendary: A boolean value to show whether the Pokémon is one of the legendary ones.
Is_Mythical: Indicates if the Pokémon is classified as a mythical creature.
Is_Ultra_Beast: Shows if the Pokémon is one of the Ultra Beasts.
number_immune: The count of move types that don’t affect the Pokémon at all.
number_not_effective: How many move types aren’t very effective against the Pokémon.
number_normal: The number of move types that have a normal effect on the Pokémon.
number_super_effective: The count of move types that are especially effective against the Pokémon.
